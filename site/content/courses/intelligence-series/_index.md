The Intelligence Series is built for practitioners who work with information for a living — investigators, analysts, cyber security professionals, and intelligence officers who need to move beyond ad hoc research and operate to a professional standard.

The curriculum covers the full tradecraft stack: structured OSINT collection methodology, rigorous source evaluation, structured analytic techniques for moving from raw findings to assessed conclusions, cognitive bias mitigation, and finished intelligence production. All courses are grounded in Australian legal and operational context and draw on 17 years of intelligence operations experience in law enforcement.

Courses in this series are suitable for people working in Australian government, law enforcement, defence, and private-sector intelligence roles. No prior formal intelligence training is assumed.
